About MediPredict

Revolutionizing healthcare accessibility through artificial intelligence

Our Mission

To democratize healthcare access by providing AI-powered disease prediction tools that help people make informed decisions about their health.

MediPredict leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze symptoms and provide preliminary health insights, bridging the gap between initial health concerns and professional medical consultation.

How Our AI Works

Data Training

Our machine learning model is trained on a comprehensive dataset containing 100+ diseases and their associated symptoms. The model learns patterns and relationships between symptoms and diseases.

AI Analysis

When you input symptoms, our Random Forest algorithm analyzes the combination and compares it against learned patterns to predict the most likely disease with a confidence score.
